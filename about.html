NACF is the National Body for all NIFES Graduates in North America, consisting of U.S and Canada. NACF North America was inaugurated in 2012 with the influence of Bro Ebere Eze, the then NIFES Director of Associates, who visited some brethren in New Jersey that year.

PURPOSE OF NACF

  • Fellowship: A greater fellowship forum on a National scale for all NIFES Alumni and Alumni groups in North America. Deut.32:30 One can chase a thousand and two will put ten thousand to flight
  • Networking: A platform for a wider interaction and opportunity to be a blessing to ourselves in the different areas of skills and professionalism with which we have been empowered
  • To Be Our Brother’s Keeper:

    NACF exists to ensure that our brother/Sisters are still

    • Standing strong in the faith
    • Winning thru their battles thru the inspiration and support of fellow brothers from the various tribes of NIFES

  • Support NIFES Headquarters Ministry and Projects: NACF exists to support NIFES headquarters vision and mission as a way of giving back to our mother fellowship through which we were raised.
  • Missions – Explore collaborative efforts with other organizations for outreach opportunities to impart our immediate communities and world at large for soul winning.

Membership

  • All Graduates of higher institutions of Nigerian Schools who attended NIFES.
  • All organized local campus Alumni groups in the United States and Canada
  • Interested believers from other Nigerian campus fellowships residing in the United States and Canada
